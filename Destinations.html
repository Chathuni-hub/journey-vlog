Sigiriya

Known as the “Lion Rock”, Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress with frescoes, gardens, and breathtaking views from the summit.

  • Climb to the summit of the rock fortress
  • Explore the ancient frescoes and mirror wall
  • Wander through the royal water gardens

Ella

A charming hill-country town surrounded by tea plantations, waterfalls, and stunning mountain views.

  • Hike to Little Adam’s Peak
  • See the iconic Nine Arches Bridge
  • Relax by Ravana Falls

Galle

A coastal city rich in history, with its Dutch fort, cobbled streets, and beautiful ocean views.

  • Walk along the ramparts of Galle Fort
  • Visit the Dutch Reformed Church
  • Relax at Unawatuna Beach nearby
